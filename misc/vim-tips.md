## convert format between 
|recipe              |action         |description                |
|--------------------|---------------|---------------------------|
|dos <--> unix       |set ff=unix    |set file-format (unix/dos) and save   |
|convert tab/space   |set et         |(no)et: expand tabs<br/> then run ``retab`` to apply convertion|
